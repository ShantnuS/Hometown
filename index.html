Top three activities to do in Birmingham

The Library of Birmingham

The ten-level Library shares a spacious entrance and foyer as well as a flexible studio theatre seating 300 people with the Birmingham Repertory Theatre.

Winterbourne House and Garden

Restored to its Edwardian Arts and Craft splendour, Winterbourne House is a unique heritage attraction set within seven acres of beautiful botanic gardens.

Edgbaston Stadium

A regular host of major domestic and international cricket, Edgbaston Stadium is known across the globe for world-class cricket.

Discover Birmingham's Rich History and Heritage

As its population swelled, the industries flourished as Birmingham became known as the “city of a thousand trades” and “the engine room of the UK”. It played a major role in the Industrial Revolution, leading the way in manufacturing thanks to its bustling factories and workshops. Ammunition, guns, metalware, tools and watches were among an array of products that were produced in Birmingham.
